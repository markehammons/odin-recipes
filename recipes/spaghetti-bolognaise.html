Spaghetti Bolognaise

Mouthwatering Spaghetti Bolognaise

This spaghetti recipe will make you the talk of the town.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Tomato concentrate
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Beef bouillon
  • Soy sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and start it to boil
  2. While the pot is reaching a boil, take a much smaller pot, and add your tomato concentrate
  3. To the tomato concentrate, add a cube of beef bouillon, a splash of red wine vinegar, a drizzle of soy sauce, and a tablespoon or two of butter
  4. Set the sauce to cook at low heat, stirring occasionally
  5. Take a frying pan, and start cooking your ground beef. Chop it up with a spatula, and again drizzle soy sauce. Cook the ground beef on medium heat
  6. Once the pot with water reaches a boil, add the spaghetti. Approximately 100g per person. Cook it for the time recommended on the package minus two minutes, and at medium high heat
  7. Once the juice is being released from the hamburger meat and it's appropriately brown throughout, put it and its juices in the tomato sauce and continue cooking at low heat
  8. Once the pasta has cooked for the recommended time minus two minutes, strain it, return it to its pot, and add the tomato sauce. Don't strain the pasta completely
  9. Cook the pasta in the sauce for about 4 more minutes, or until the sauce has thickened enough to stick to the pasta
  10. Serve!

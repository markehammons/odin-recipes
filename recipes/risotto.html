Chicken Risotto

Creamy Chicken Risotto

This chicken risotto will be the envy of your neighborhood. It's cook time is long, but it's well worth the wait!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Arborio Rice
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Chicken Broth
  • White wine
  • Chopped Mushroom
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan Cheese (freshly grated or sliced)
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring a quantity of chicken broth to boil (don't worry about making too much, worry about making too little). You can use chicken bouillon instead
  2. In a separate pot chop up mushrooms and garlic and simmer them at medium heat in olive oil. Only use enough oil to coat the bottom of the pan
  3. Once the chicken broth is ready reduce the heat on the mushroom and garlic, and add some butter
  4. Measure out about 75g of risotto per person you'll be serving and add to the first pot
  5. After cooking the rice/mushroom/garlic mixture at medium heat for about 3 minutes, reduce the heat on the mixture to medium-low
  6. Add wine to the mixture and chopped parsley. You should add enough to halfway soak the rice you added
  7. Start ladling chicken broth into the rice/mushroom/garlic mixture. Use one ladle-full at a time, and wait for the rice to absorb the liquid till it's a bit sticky before adding more. Stir as the mixture is being absorbed to keep anything from burning
  8. Continue the above step for about 25 minutes
  9. Start cooking the chicken breast on high temperature in a seperate pan, stirring and flipping the chicken to get it evenly cooked
  10. Once the chicken is releasing its juices, reduce the temperature and keep it warm
  11. While the chicken is cooking, keep ladeling broth into the rice mixture for another 9 minutes
  12. Add the chicken and parmesan to the mixture. I typically add half a wedge of parmesan (60g) for two people, but you add as per your tastes. Stir for the next three minutes, until the risotto starts getting sticky
  13. While stirring, taste the cream from the risotto. If it's not flavorful enough, add salt until it tastes good to you
  14. Serve hot!
